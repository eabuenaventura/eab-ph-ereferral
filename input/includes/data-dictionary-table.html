These are data elements that remain fixed throughout the referral process (the “root” of the referral). For example: Referring Practitioner, Initiating Facility, Reason for referral. All Receiving data is represented by a Task. These are data elements that are moving/dynamic -- may change as the referral is acted upon. For example: Receiving facility, care navigator, time called. Acceptance or rejection of the referral may change these values.
